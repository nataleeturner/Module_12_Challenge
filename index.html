This site allows anyone to choose a test subject and quickly see a dashboard containing information about the bacteria collected from their belly botton. First you can see the demographic info of the test subject. To the right of this you can see the top 10 bacteria cultures found in their belly button. Next, you can see how frequently the test subject washes their belly botton. Finally, below this information there is a bubble chart with details about all of the different bacteria found in the test subject's belly button.
